Welcome

As a doctoral candidate at ENSIAS | UM5, I am deeply engrossed in the exploration of software engineering optimization and software reverse engineering. My passion as a researcher in software reverse engineering lies in dissecting the construction of software, pinpointing potential enhancements in current software development methodologies. This includes scrutinizing code to detect patterns, vulnerabilities, and other susceptibilities, and leveraging this understanding to innovate new tools.
